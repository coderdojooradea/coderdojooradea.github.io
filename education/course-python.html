Description

Hey there! From 24th of February Coder Dojo Oradea is starting fresh new sessions on Python Programming. This one is sponsored by Fundatia Comunitarea Oradea through Fondul Stiintescu. If you want to participate, please make sure you read the details below.

Why do we think it's important to learn to code?

  • Well, it helps you think in a more structural way
  • It gives you the opportunity to create stuff very fast
  • And it's also FUN!

What do you need in order to register?

  • As you might have noticed, english is required! All online courses will be in english!
  • Familiar with the following concepts in math: variables, arithmetics, fractions
  • And the most important one, the will to learn new things!
  • Be atmost 17 years old :)

How will a tipical session look like?

  • We'll follow different online courses on Python Programming
  • You'll be given different problems to solve and you'll get help where you need it
  • You can pick your own project to work on by yourself or with a colleague - Team work is appreciated!
  • You can check out and discuss ideas with your colleages which is very engaging

Do I need to bring something with me?

  • Yes! Please don't forget your laptop as you'll use it during the sessions!
  • Bring also a mouse, it makes for a better experience as the touch pad is not that great when you're writting code!
